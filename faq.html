Frequently Asked Questions

Admissions

1. How can I apply for SSC, HSC, or BA/BSS programs?

To apply for SSC or HSC programs, visit osapsnew.bou.ac.bd, select the Open School option, and click "Apply Now" for the desired program. Choose your subjects, complete the online payment, and submit required documents to the designated school/college. For BA/BSS programs, follow the same process and submit documents to the BOU office in your district. For other programs, check the admission circular in the Academic Info > Admission menu or the "View Details" section on the OSAPS website.

[](https://www.bou.ac.bd/About/FAQ)

2. What should I do if I entered incorrect information during the online application?

Contact the OSAPS Helpline at 01618-977237 or 01907-451612 (Sunday to Thursday, 8:30 AM to 3:30 PM) or email help.osaps@bou.ac.bd to request corrections.

[](https://bougovbd.com/faqs)

3. Can students with a 4-year diploma apply for the BA/BSS program?

Yes, students who have completed a 4-year diploma equivalent to HSC can apply for the BA/BSS program.

[](https://www.bou.ac.bd/About/FAQ)

4. How do I find out if admissions are open for a specific program?

Check the Academic Info > Admission menu on bou.ac.bd for the latest admission circular or visit osapsnew.bou.ac.bd and review the "Offered Programs" section.

[](https://bougovbd.com/faqs)

Study Centers

5. Where are the study centers for LLB, BA/BSS Honors, or Master’s programs?

LLB and BA/BSS Honors programs are currently offered only at the BOU Dhaka Regional Center. Master’s programs may have additional study centers; check osapsnew.bou.ac.bd during the application process for a list of study centers in your selected district. You can also contact your district’s BOU office for details.

[](https://bougovbd.com/faqs)

6. How can I find study centers for SSC/HSC programs in my district?

During the online application on osapsnew.bou.ac.bd, select your district to view a list of available study centers or schools. Alternatively, contact your local BOU office for a list of study centers.

[](https://www.bou.ac.bd/About/FAQ)

Examinations and Results

7. How can I check my exam results?

Visit result.bou.ac.bd to view published results for various programs. You can also check the Results section on bou.ac.bd for result news and downloadable PDF files.

[](https://www.bou.ac.bd/)

8. I was admitted long ago, and my profile shows an active status. Can I still take exams?

Contact the OSAPS Helpline (help.osaps@bou.ac.bd) or your regional BOU office to confirm your eligibility and registration status for exams.

[](https://www.bou.ac.bd/About/FAQ)

Technical Support

9. Who should I contact for online admission or registration issues?

For issues within 15 working days, contact the OSAPS Helpline at 01618-977237 or 01907-451612 (Sunday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM), email help.osaps@bou.ac.bd, or visit your regional/sub-regional center. For specific program issues, contact the program’s designated programmer or email the ICT Head at icthead@bou.ac.bd.

[](https://www.bou.ac.bd/CitizenCharter/BOUPromisedService)
