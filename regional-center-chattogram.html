Chattogram Regional Center

About the Center

Welcome to the tourist city of Chattogram and its glittering beauty Regional Center (RC) of Bangladesh Open University (BOU). Since its inception, it has been imparting education through the Open and Distance Learning (ODL) system with a strong reputation. Currently, approximately 55,000 learners, who were previously dropped from the education stream, are benefiting from education at this center. They are enrolled in 16 formal programs and studying at 128 study centers across Chattogram and its adjoining districts.

Programs Offered

Most learners are studying in programs such as SSC, HSC, BA, BSS, CMBA, MBA, BEd, and MEd. Other programs include BAgEd, CEd, BBA, BBS, PGDM, DCSA, CPFP, and CEMPA. Additionally, the newly introduced NICHE-01 program, designed for Army, Navy, and Air Force recruits, has added a new dimension to education at this center.

Location and Facilities

The Chattogram RC office is located at the Central Railway Building (CRB) area, adjacent to Chattogram Stadium, Kotwali, Chattogram. This scenic location is just 20 km from the Bay of Bengal's shoreline, enhanced by nearby hills. The office operates from a three-story building, with office rooms and classrooms on the second floor and rest houses on the third floor, covering an area of 0.82 acres.
