Dhaka Regional Center

About the Center

Welcome to Dhaka Regional Resources Center (RRC) of Bangladesh Open University (BOU). It is one of the most important RRCs of BOU among its 12 RRCs. According to the main city of the region and the location of the RRC office, it is named as Dhaka RRC. The BOU authority decided to establish this RRC to assure availability of BOU resources and activities in the region through its Coordinating Offices and Study Centers.
